    Digitald comes in as a realtime solution to drunk-driving which is a major cause of road accidents. It's an automated system hence there's no need for the availability of the traffic police officer since it has a sensor which senses the alcohol levels automatically.It is also a solution towards reducing the spread of covid 19 since it does away with the breathelizing aspect.
